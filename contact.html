Common Questions

Why can't the chat connect? ▼
Make sure Docker Desktop is running, AnythingLLM is up on port 3001, and LM Studio's local server is active on port 1234. Serve the HTML file via python -m http.server 8080 to avoid CORS issues.
I got a 403 error. What does that mean? ▼
A 403 means your API key is invalid or missing. Open the Settings panel in the chat demo, and enter the correct API key from your AnythingLLM dashboard.
Is my conversation data safe? ▼
Yes. Everything runs locally on your machine. No data is sent to external servers. Your conversations never leave your device. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
Can I use MediAssist AI for professional medical decisions? ▼
No. MediAssist AI is a general information tool only. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a licensed healthcare provider.
