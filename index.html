Process

How MediAssist AI Works

A clean four-stage pipeline from user query to medically grounded response.

1

User Query

The user types a medical question or describes symptoms in natural language through the chat interface.

2

RAG Retrieval

AnythingLLM embeds the query and retrieves the most relevant chunks from the medical document knowledge base.

3

LLM Processing

The retrieved context + question is passed to the local LM Studio model (Mistral-7B or Llama-3.1) for response generation.

4

Safe Response

The answer is returned with appropriate disclaimers, source citations, and guidance to seek professional care when warranted.

Responsible AI

Built with Ethics at the Core

Medical AI carries unique responsibilities. MediAssist AI was designed with safeguards across every layer of the system.

⚖️

Bias Mitigation

Training data was curated from diverse, peer-reviewed sources to reduce demographic and diagnostic bias. The model is regularly evaluated against known medical bias benchmarks.

🔒

Privacy by Design

Running entirely locally via LM Studio, no patient data is transmitted externally. All conversations are processed on-device, ensuring HIPAA-aware data handling.

🎯

Scope Boundaries

The system is explicitly designed to recognize the limits of AI medical advice, always redirecting users to qualified professionals for diagnoses, prescriptions, or emergencies.

📋

Source Transparency

Responses cite the specific document chunks retrieved by the RAG system, so users can trace information back to its source and make informed judgments about reliability.

🚨

Emergency Detection

The model is tuned to recognize emergency symptoms (chest pain, stroke signs, severe allergic reactions) and immediately directs users to call emergency services.

📊

Continuous Evaluation

Performance is tracked against accuracy, hallucination rate, and user feedback metrics. The knowledge base is updated with current guidelines as they are released.
