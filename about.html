🏥 Our Story

About MediAssist AI

We're building the future of accessible health information — responsibly, privately, and with humanity at the center of every decision.

Our Mission

Making Medical Knowledge Accessible to All

MediAssist AI was born from a simple observation: millions of people turn to the internet for health answers every day, yet they're met with unreliable sources, confusing jargon, and information that may not apply to their situation.

We set out to build something different — a domain-specific medical AI that delivers accurate, contextually grounded health information using retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), while being radically transparent about its limitations.

Our system runs entirely locally on your machine. No servers. No data collection. No compromise on your privacy. Just honest, helpful health information when you need it.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Every design decision in MediAssist AI flows from these principles.

🔒

Privacy First

Your health conversations are deeply personal. We designed MediAssist AI to run entirely on-device — no conversation ever leaves your machine.

🎯

Radical Honesty

We are transparent about what AI can and cannot do in healthcare. We actively encourage users to consult real medical professionals.

⚖️

Equity in Health

We curate training data from diverse sources to minimize bias and ensure our assistant serves all users fairly, regardless of background.

📋

Source Transparency

Every response cites its source documents. You always know where the information came from and can evaluate it yourself.

🚨

Safety as Default

When in doubt, we err on the side of caution — always directing users toward professional care for anything that could be serious.

🔬

Evidence-Based

Our knowledge base is built exclusively on peer-reviewed literature, clinical guidelines, and reputable medical reference documents.

Under the Hood

The Technology

MediAssist AI is built on a carefully chosen stack designed for accuracy, privacy, and reliability.

🧠

LM Studio

Runs Mistral-7B or Llama-3.1 locally on your hardware. All model inference happens on-device with zero external API calls.

📚

AnythingLLM + RAG

Retrieval-augmented generation pulls precise, cited answers from our curated medical document knowledge base — reducing hallucination.

🐳

Docker Containerized

The entire stack ships as a Docker setup for reproducible, platform-independent deployment on Windows, macOS, and Linux.
