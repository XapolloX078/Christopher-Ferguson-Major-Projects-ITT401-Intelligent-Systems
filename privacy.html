01 Information We Collect

MediAssist AI is designed to operate without collecting personal information. Because the application runs locally on your machine via LM Studio and AnythingLLM, there is no backend server that receives your data.

What We Do Not Collect

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Your health questions, symptoms, or medical history
  • Your IP address or device identifiers
  • Usage analytics, session duration, or behavioral data
  • Location data of any kind

Static Website Analytics (Optional)

If you access the MediAssist AI landing page through a hosted web server, standard web server access logs may record your IP address and the pages you visit. These logs are used solely for diagnosing server issues and are not used for tracking or advertising purposes.

02 Local Processing

All AI processing in MediAssist AI occurs on your local machine. When you type a question into the chat interface:

  • Your query is sent to AnythingLLM running on localhost:3001
  • AnythingLLM performs RAG retrieval from your local document store
  • The question and context are sent to LM Studio running on localhost:1234
  • The response is returned to your browser — all within your local network

No part of this pipeline involves external servers. Your conversation stays on your machine from start to finish.

03 No Data Transmission

🔒 MediAssist AI does not transmit your data to Anthropic, OpenAI, or any other AI provider. The language model runs locally.

Unlike cloud-based AI assistants, MediAssist AI does not send your health queries to third-party servers for processing. This means:

  • Your medical questions cannot be used to train external AI models
  • Your data cannot be subpoenaed from a server we don't operate
  • There is no data breach risk from an external provider
  • Your conversations are as private as anything else on your local computer

04 Cookies & Analytics

The MediAssist AI interface, when served locally, does not use cookies or any client-side tracking technology. We do not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or any similar analytics service in the core application.

If you deploy MediAssist AI as a hosted web application, you are responsible for ensuring your hosting configuration complies with applicable cookie and privacy laws.

05 Third-Party Services

MediAssist AI uses the following open-source components, all of which run locally:

  • AnythingLLM — Open source, locally hosted. anythingllm.com
  • LM Studio — Desktop application, locally hosted. lmstudio.ai
  • Docker — Containerization, locally hosted

Please review the privacy policies of each of these tools separately. We are not responsible for their data practices, though note that all three are designed for local use.

06 Data Security

Because data never leaves your machine, security is primarily in your hands. We recommend:

  • Keeping your operating system and Docker updated
  • Not exposing your AnythingLLM port (3001) to the public internet
  • Using a strong, unique API key for AnythingLLM authentication
  • Encrypting your system drive if you want conversation history to be protected at rest

07 Children's Privacy

⚠️ MediAssist AI is not designed for use by children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used MediAssist AI, please note that no data is retained, so there is nothing to delete. However, we encourage you to supervise minors' use of AI health tools.

08 Your Rights

Because we collect no personal data, traditional data rights (access, erasure, portability) are automatically satisfied — there is nothing for us to provide, delete, or export on your behalf.

If you have conversation history stored locally by AnythingLLM, you can clear it at any time through the AnythingLLM interface or by deleting the relevant Docker volume.

09 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of MediAssist AI after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

We will never introduce data collection without prominent disclosure and, where required by law, your explicit consent.

10 Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please reach out through our Contact Support page. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours.
